<p><ul><li>Solid Form</li><li>Semi-solid Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic cocoa liquor market is categorized into solid and semi-solid forms. Solid form cocoa liquor is typically produced by pressing cocoa beans, resulting in a dense chocolate mass that can be molded or grated for various applications, including baking and confectionery. In contrast, semi-solid form cocoa liquor retains some moisture, making it easier to blend and incorporate into products like creamy sauces, beverages, and chocolate coatings. Both forms cater to diverse consumer preferences and culinary uses in the food industry.</p></p>
