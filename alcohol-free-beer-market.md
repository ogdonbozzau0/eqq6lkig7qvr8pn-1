<p><strong>The Alcohol free Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fermentation-limited Method</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol-free beer market is primarily categorized into two types based on production methods: fermentation-limited and dealcoholization. The fermentation-limited method involves minimal fermentation, controlling yeast action to restrict alcohol formation while maintaining flavor. In contrast, the dealcoholization method involves brewing regular beer and then removing alcohol through various techniques like distillation or filtration, ensuring the beverage retains its original taste profile. Both methods cater to the growing demand for non-alcoholic options, appealing to health-conscious consumers and those preferring moderate choices.</p></p>
