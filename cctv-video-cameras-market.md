<p><strong>The CCTV Video Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Cameras</li><li>IP Cameras</li><li>Hybrid Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCTV video camera market is primarily categorized into three types: Analog Cameras, IP Cameras, and Hybrid Cameras. Analog cameras transmit video signals via coaxial cables and are known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. IP cameras, on the other hand, utilize internet protocol for video transmission, offering high-resolution images and remote access capabilities. Hybrid cameras combine features of both analog and IP technologies, allowing for greater flexibility in surveillance setups and enabling users to transition between systems as needed.</p></p>
