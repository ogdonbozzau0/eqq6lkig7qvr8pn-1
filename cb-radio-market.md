<p><p>The CB radio market is divided into two main types: handheld and fixed CB radios. Handheld CB radios are portable devices designed for mobility, making them ideal for outdoor activities, travel, and emergency communications. They typically feature rechargeable batteries and compact designs. Fixed CB radios, on the other hand, are installed in vehicles or at home, offering higher power output and extended range. They often come with advanced features, including larger displays and enhanced audio quality, suitable for more extensive communication needs.</p></p>
