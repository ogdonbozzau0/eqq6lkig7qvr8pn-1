<p><p>The fruit juice beverage stabilizers market includes several key types that enhance texture and shelf life. Xanthan gum is a polysaccharide that thickens and stabilizes, while carrageenan, derived from seaweed, provides gelling properties. Gum Arabic, a natural gum, acts as an emulsifier and stabilizer. Carboxymethyl cellulose (CMC) is a cellulose derivative that improves viscosity and prevents separation. Other stabilizers may include locust bean gum or guar gum, catering to diverse formulations and consumer preferences in the beverage industry.</p></p>
