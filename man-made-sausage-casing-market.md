<p><p>The man-made sausage casing market comprises various types, including collagen, cellulose, plastic, and others. Collagen casings, derived from animal hides, provide a natural texture and are widely used in fresh and cooked sausages. Cellulose casings are made from plant-based materials, ideal for skinless sausages and offer uniformity and strength. Plastic casings are used for specific applications like vacuum-packed products, ensuring durability and moisture retention. Other casings may include fibrous or edible varieties, catering to diverse processing and consumer preferences.</p></p>
