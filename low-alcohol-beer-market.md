<p><strong>The Low alcohol Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Limit Fermentation</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low alcohol beer market includes two primary production methods: limit fermentation and dealcoholization. Limit fermentation involves controlling the fermentation process to produce beer with naturally lower alcohol content, typically below 0.5% ABV. In contrast, the dealcoholization method begins with traditional brewing and then removes alcohol through processes like vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both methods cater to health-conscious consumers seeking flavorful alternatives with reduced alcohol content while maintaining the taste and quality of traditional beers.</p></p>
